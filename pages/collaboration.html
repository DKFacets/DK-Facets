Our Collaboration Model

A transparent, commission-based partnership designed for jewelers who value trust, flexibility, and long-term growth.

Built for Long-Term Partnerships

D&K Facets operates as a coordination and consulting partner — not a traditional supplier. Our collaboration model is designed to remove upfront risk, simplify execution, and support jewelers as their business scales.

Core Principles

01

Transparency

Clear communication, documented approvals, and no hidden costs at any stage.

02

Confidentiality

Your designs, pricing, and client details remain strictly protected.

03

Consistency

Reliable coordination standards across every order and every market.

Commission-Based Model

No Upfront Cost

You do not pay any onboarding or setup fees. Collaboration begins only when real orders are placed.

Aligned Incentives

Our success is directly linked to your success. We earn when value is delivered.

Scalable Support

Whether you place occasional orders or manage large volumes, the model adapts.
